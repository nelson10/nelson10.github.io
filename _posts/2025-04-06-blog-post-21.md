Attendance at ICASSP 2025 conference in Hyderabad, India
======

Dr. Nelson Diaz participated in International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing 2025 conference in Hyderabad, India, from the 6th to the 11th of April 2025. Nelson  presented an oral talk entitled "Phase Retrieval by Designed Hadamard Complementary Coded Apertures", in the Workshop: Synthetic Apertures in Computational Imaging [[workshop_ICASSP]](https://sites.google.com/view/saciworkshop/home), [[DOI]](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10163979).
