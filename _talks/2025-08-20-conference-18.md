[[Paper]](https://nelson10.github.io/files/Conference18.pdf), [[Link]](https://opg.optica.org/abstract.cfm?uri=3D-2025-JW2A.7), [[Poster]](https://nelson10.github.io/files/PosterCOSI2025.pdf)

This work presents a multispectral-aware diffractive optical element design for extended depth-of-field imaging. By using a data-driven stochastic optimization approach, the proposed method enhances spectral fidelity and depth invariance, outperforming state-of-the-art methods.
